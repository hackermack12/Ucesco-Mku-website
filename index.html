About UCESCO

UCESCO CLUB is a student-led organization at Mount Kenya University dedicated to making a positive impact on local communities,its Mission is to support fellow students to boost their knowledge in skills By empowering them to take charge of their future.It aims at building a path to sustainable growth for students in the sorrounding communities.It is based in Flt33 on thursday.New members are given high quality mentorship to develop and be holistic.
